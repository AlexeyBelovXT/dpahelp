Roles and Tasks

Производственные процессы и роли в DPA:
CEO
- the analysis of progress in comparison with the last periods (reports, KPI, OEE and an automated workplace);
- the integrated production work indicators;
- the analysis of bottlenecks and unplanned idle times in the system of production;
- availability of the RTs resources;
System engineer 
- the system is simple in installation, setup, support and scaling;
- full compatibility with the existing IT systems of the enterprise;
- compliance to the selected IT-policy, to the DBMS types and security policy;
- possibility of distribution of service load to several servers;
- detailed setup of the client (system as Web service);
workshop foreman
- operational information about production;
- red line messages about deviations in process;
- an automatic creation of reports for the period and notifications;
- an opportunity to quickly remove the causes of idle times;
- visualization of the actual loading;
technologist
- technological parameters in real time;
- support of technical cards;
- library and version check of control program;
- loading of technical cards from PLM;
- proofreading and comparison of the executed code of control program with reference;
- portable version;
standardizer
- forming of norms on production history;
- comparison of reference and actual norms;
- messages about deviations;
- Loading of values from PLM;
operator
- control of the current productivity (ANDON - display in workshop, the screen of the operator);
- an operative communication with chief and the technologist; 
- possibility of remote loading and unloading control program;    
- automatic accounting of production output;
- order of materials and furniture online;
supervisor (dispatcher)
- control over the implementation of planned daily tasks of workshop;
- control and analysis of a current status of productions;
- rapid response to unplanned events;
mechanic
- data on an equipment operating time (engine hours) for the purpose of M&R (
Maintenance and Repair) and preventive maintenance;
- control of critical duties;
- analysis of vibrations of the machine;
- errors and incredible values of parameters;
- electronic regulations of service;
- diagram and control of repairs;
 - reduction of terms of service and maintenance of TOIR and diagnostics;
