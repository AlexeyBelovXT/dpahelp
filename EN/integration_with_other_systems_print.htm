integration with other systems

 
Integration allows to get rid of duplication of data in several information systems. Helps to support information in current state. Allows to differentiate accesses to systems and at the same time to save upgradeability of data.
 
For example, if the product range is already created in the ERP system of the enterprise and is supported in current state, then superfluous will bring the same product range repeatedly to other system, for example DPA. Thanks to integration DPA can load the nomenclature. Also it is possible to prohibit change of the nomenclature in DPA to any users to avoid errors in the report and worker processes.
