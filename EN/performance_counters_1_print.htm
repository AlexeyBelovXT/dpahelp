Performance counters

 
For work with performance counters it is necessary to select the corresponding node in a tree and to go to the Performance counters tab.
Monitoring. Performance counters
Click "Select counters" , select needed counters and click .
Performance counters. Selection
Specify necessary parameters in "Scale of Counters" window, and click .
Performance counters. Scale selection
Click "Save" to apply selected settings.
