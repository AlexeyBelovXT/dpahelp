About DPA

 
DPA (Discrete Processes Automation автоматизация дискретных процессов) - it is the analytical system watching a status of CNC machines in real time. It is connected "from a box" to a considerable part of the equipment existing in the market. Without completion and long adjustment. 
 
Digital production:
· Data from CNC in real time. The DPA system collects and processes machine data. Helps to manage production and to support the equipment in proper condition. ​ Modern CNC allow to connect the equipment to DPA via the interface provided by vendors.
· Tools of the analysis and control​. The preset and configured algorithms provide interaction of personnel, provide control of deviations on production. The current status of the equipment, last events are available to the analysis. The predicative analysis is available – the System analyzes the taken place events and issues the recommendations or forecasts helping to avoid emergency situations.
· Development of a production system ​. Installation of MDC allows to receive new benefits from investments in information systems and the equipment and also to involve new features of digital production.
 
DPA Assignment:
· Careful operation of the equipment ​. The system monitors the available equipment status parameters to prevent critical modes. Monitors alarm messages and informs the user. The module of electronic regulations of servicing orders work of Maintenance and Repair Service. ​
· Exact respect for technology ​. Technologists control compliance of technologies and the actual processing (execution of the operating program) by means of technical cards and routes. ​
· Improved production and quality. Control of tasks assigned to work centers, manual and automatic regulation of operations, automatic control of processing, allow to plan and monitor production for compliance with plans and prevent downtime.
 
The classic goals of the system implementation are:
· Improve equipment efficiency. The system provides users with objective and up-to-date information about the state of the devices and the progress of production. Control efficiency is increased.
· Retention of investments. The system is integrated into the existing information space of the enterprise. The CNC capabilities are fully used.
 
 
DPA functionality:
· Analysis of collected data and display of results in the form of graphs, 3D models, volume diagrams;
· Real-time data management. Automatic processing of emergency messages, signals and events;
· Setting time limits for operations, resource requirements. Formation of missing norms and technologies on production history;
· Control program execution check. Comparison of the version loaded into the system with the version executed on the equipment;
· Real-time control of deviations from manufacturing technology. Immediate response and notification of employee;
· Integration with maintenance and R & D systems. Minimization of equipment downtime during repair;
· Selection of plan optimization criteria and methods, integration with various analytical platforms and management systems (APS and BI systems);
· Built-in Event Knowledge Base;
· Delineation of user access rights, including by staffing table;
· Create report forms.
