Working time

 
For the enterprise level, the production site and department it is possible to set working hours.
For setup select the corresponding structure item, in the line "Working hours" click "Edit".
 
                                                                                                                                                 Selected item of structure
 
In the opened window select a day of the week or specific date, press "add rule" .
